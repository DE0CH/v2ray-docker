We investigate infinity with set theory, which deals with sets - collections of things, like the set of all natural numbers and the set of all polygons. Sets can contain anything (including sets) and can be however large. But even though a set can be easily larger than the entire physical (not just the observable) universe, there are sets that are too large to be a set.
